About the App

Onit is a furniture marketplace designed to make buying and selling second-hand furniture easier and more accessible. Users can browse listings, connect directly with sellers, or post requests for specific items they are looking for. By combining traditional marketplace features with a request-based system, Onit helps users discover furniture that matches their space, style, and budget while creating a safer and more convenient buying experience.
